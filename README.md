My day to day setup!

## Information

- **OS:** [Origami Linux](https://origami.wf) (A immutable distro I contributes to and daily drive!)
- **DE:** [Cosmic](https://system76.com/cosmic)
- **WM:** [I3wm](https://i3wm.org) & [hyprland](https://hyprland.org)
- **Terminal:** [Foot](https://codeberg.org/dnkl/foot)
- **Shell:** [Fish](https://fishshell.com)
- **Editor:** [Neovim](https://neovim.io)

I use GNU [stow](https://www.gnu.org/software/stow/stow.html) to managing my symlinks.
